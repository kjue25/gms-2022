    About our product

    We make blankets that are hand-knit with care. Our blankets are made with chenille yarn and come in a variety of colors. We chose this yarn because of the remarkable softness and the pure fluffiness. The blankets come in light airy colors, such as light grey, light blue, white, teal, and navy blue. there is also a mixed option with the yarn. One blanket is four feet by five feet, the perfect size for a lap blanket.
