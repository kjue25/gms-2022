Why Flicker?

Our products have many enticing qualities. We handmake our candles using paraffin wax and fragrance oils that make our candles smell amazing. Once you finish using your candle, you can repurpose your jar to use it for many different things. Our candles come in 4 pretty colors that match our scents (in order from left to right above)- lavender, appleblossom, vanilla, and honeysuckle.

The candles we make bring a new, calm feel to any space, and brighten up any room. We use only high quality ingredients that make our candles cute, aesthetic, and make the colors and scents last for an extended period of time. We hope you enjoy our candles!
