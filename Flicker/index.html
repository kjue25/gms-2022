Hi! We are Flicker! Our product for Entreprenurial class is candles! These candles are great for relaxing and they smell delightful.

We handmake our candles to ensure your satisfaction, and to ensure that our products are high quality.

Our candles come in multiple colors and scents (from left to right, first four pictures)- lavender (purple), appleblossom (pink/red), vanilla (no color/ white), and honeysuckle (yellow).

Our candles bring a new, calm feel to any space, and will relax you with their amazing scents. We use paraffin wax and fragrance oils for our candles that make the scent and color last for an extended period of time. We hope you enjoy our candles!
