Welcome to our Back-to-Nature Notebooks website! We are a group of three working on spreading mindfulness and creativity throughout our community. Meet our creators:

Charlie - VP of Operations Noor - VP of Communications + Finance Giselle - VP of Marketing Liana - VP of Information

We created Back-to-Nature Notebooks because we wanted to spread mindfullness and creativity. Getting out in nature with a notebook can lead to endless possibilities. We wanted everyone to experience the serenity of being outside with nothing but yourself and your inspiration. We designed our notebooks to be smaller so you can travel with it, and wherever and whenever an idea pops up, you can write it down so you don't forget it later. When you invest in our nature notebooks, you are investing in a natural world.
