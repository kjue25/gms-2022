Simpli Bright

Our company sells bubble candles and cloud candles! The bubble candles are the larger kind and come individually, and the cloud candles come in 3 sizes in a pack.

Our candles are also different colors. We chose pastel colors so they are cute and aesthetically pleasing, we will have many color options. Our candles will also have many scents. We hope you buy our product and promise you that you will be satisfied with your purchase.

WARNING: Please be careful when you light our candles! It is best burned if there is a plate of some sort underneath the candle so it can catch the hot wax. Please also be informed that our candles will look different after you light it because of the wax melting, so it's recommended to use only for decoration and it looks at its best when it is not melted. THANK YOU!
